Franchise Management System - Project Overview

This project is a Franchise Management System designed to streamline the operations, subscription, and management of franchise businesses. It provides a centralized platform for franchisors, franchisees, single stores, and staff to manage business processes, subscriptions, and communications efficiently.

User Roles

  • Franchisor: The owner or manager of the franchise brand. Manages franchisees, subscription plans, and oversees business operations at a network level. Does not directly operate franchisee stores.
  • Franchisee: The operator of an individual franchise unit. Manages their own store, subscribes to plans, and interacts with the franchisor.
  • Single Store: An independent store owner who subscribes to the platform without being part of a franchise network. Has both the setup/branding functions of a franchisor and the operational functions of a franchisee (e.g., manage staff, quotes, etc.).
  • Staff: Employees who assist in daily operations, either at the franchisor, franchisee, or single store level, with limited access based on permissions.
Important:
Single Store and Franchisor are both considered tenants in the system, but their functions are different.
  • Single Store has initial and necessary functions like a franchisor (e.g., setup branding, products, etc.) and also has full operational functions like a franchisee (e.g., manage staff, quotes, daily operations).
  • Franchisor manages franchisees at a high level (network, standards, plans) and does not directly operate franchisee stores.

Main Business Purpose

The main business of this project is to provide a SaaS platform for franchise and single store management, enabling franchisors to offer subscription-based services to franchisees, and allowing single store owners to manage their business independently. The system automates tenant (franchisee and single store) onboarding, subscription billing, payment processing, and authority management, reducing manual work and improving transparency.

Main Features

  • Subscription Management: Flexible subscription plans for franchisees and single stores, with automated billing and payment tracking.
  • Tenant (Franchisee & Single Store) Onboarding: Guided onboarding flow for new franchisees and single store owners, including account creation, plan selection, and payment setup.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Authority and permission management for franchisors, franchisees, single store owners, and staff.
  • Payment Integration: Support for multiple payment methods (credit card, PayPal, digital wallets) and secure payment processing.
  • Business Analytics: Dashboard and reporting tools for tracking subscription status, revenue, and operational metrics.
  • Automated Communication: Email notifications for onboarding, payment success, and account management.
  • Multi-Tenant Support: Each franchisee and single store operates as a separate tenant with isolated data and management.

Sites Overview

  • Franchise-web: Platform for Franchise Owners to create new tenants, subscribe to plans, and manage payments. Franchise Owners only manage the franchise in general (e.g., tenant information, tenant admin, tenant plan), not day-to-day operations.
  • Tenant-web: Dedicated subdomain for each tenant after creation. Used by tenant admins, franchisee admins, and staff to operate and control their tenant. Most tenant operations (store management, staff, quotes, etc.) happen here.
  • Super Admin-web: Site for Super Admins to control the system at a high level. Main features include managing other Super Admins and tenants. Note: Super Admins do not control all users in this MVP.
